## Problem

Organizations needed a centralized portal for employee authentication, time tracking, and leave management workflows.

## Solution

Developed a corporate portal supporting:

- User authentication and authorization
- Working hours tracking
- Leave management workflows with approval chains

Deployed end-to-end on AWS using CloudFormation and Amplify for scalable, serverless hosting.

## Tech Stack

Vue.js, AWS Amplify, CloudFormation, Serverless Architecture
