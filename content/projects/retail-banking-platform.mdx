## Problem

A major Pakistani bank needed a modern digital banking platform to serve 375+ branches with real-time payment capabilities and comprehensive financial modules.

## Solution

Contributed to retail banking platform development with core modules including:

- **RAAST P2P/P2M** — Register, link/delink, fetch title, transactions
- **IBFT & Fund Transfers** — Interbank payment network integration
- **Card Management** — Order, activation (HOT/WARM/COLD), PIN changes, POS/ATM/ECOM
- **Utility Bill Payments** — Government fees, education, gas, electricity
- **Wallet Top-up & Telecom Services** — Recharge, bill payment, bundle subscriptions
- **Savings Pot & PFM** — Virtual accounts, spending visualization with charts
- **Notifications** — Email, SMS, and push notifications

## Outcomes

Delivered high-availability backend services enabling seamless real-time financial transactions across 375+ branches.

## Tech Stack

Java, Spring Boot, Temenos, MySQL, Microservices, REST APIs
